♠ called
called / kC:ld /
◙ adj. [not before noun]
• having a particular name

  他们的儿子叫什么名字？

  我不认识叫斯科特的人。
 »I've forgotten what the firm he works for is called.
  我已经忘记他所在公司的名称。
 »What's it called again? Yeah, that's right. A modem.
  再说一遍它叫什么？好的,对,调制解调器。
